# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

## Introduction

Thank you for using Nearon.

Nearon respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be accessed by the application and how it is used.

## Information We Access

Nearon may access the following information:

### Bluetooth Data

The app uses Bluetooth to discover and identify nearby Bluetooth-enabled devices.

### Location Data

iOS requires location permission to enable Bluetooth scanning and device discovery features. Nearon uses location information only for Bluetooth-related functionality and device mapping features.

### Purchase Information

If you purchase a subscription or in-app purchase, Apple provides transaction information necessary to verify and restore purchases.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:

- Discover nearby Bluetooth devices
- Display signal strength and estimated distance
- Track saved devices
- Provide device location estimates
- Verify subscriptions and purchases
- Restore previous purchases

## Data Storage

Nearon stores saved devices and preferences locally on your device.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information.

## Third-Party Services

Nearon may use Apple services including:

- App Store
- StoreKit
- CoreBluetooth
- CoreLocation

These services are governed by Apple's own policies and terms.

## Data Sharing

Nearon does not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share personal information with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

Nearon is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

## Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information stored by the application.

## Disclaimer

Bluetooth signal strength, distance estimates, directional guidance, and map projections are estimates and may not always be accurate.

## Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.
